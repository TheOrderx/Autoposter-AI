Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 11, 2026

Autoposter AI ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use the Autoposter AI application and our integrations with TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube APIs.

1. Information We Collect

When you authorize Autoposter AI via OAuth, we may access the following information:

  • Account Profiles: Your username, display name, and profile picture (via `user.info.basic`) to identify your account within our dashboard.
  • Content Metadata: Information about the videos and images you upload for the purpose of scheduling and publishing.
  • Authentication Tokens: Secure OAuth access tokens required to perform actions (like uploading and publishing) on your behalf. These are encrypted and stored securely.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected information strictly for the following purposes:

  • Automated Publishing: To upload and publish your content to TikTok, Instagram, or YouTube as requested by you.
  • AI Optimization: To analyze your video content and generate relevant captions, tags, and SEO metadata using AI services (e.g., Google Gemini).
  • User Experience: To display your connected accounts in the application interface so you can manage multiple profiles easily.

3. Data Security & Retention

Your security is our top priority. All access tokens are stored in an encrypted format. We do not store your raw passwords. We only retain the data necessary to provide our services, and you can revoke our access at any time through the respective platform's security settings.

4. Third-Party Services

Autoposter AI interacts with several third-party APIs. By using our tool, you also agree to be bound by their respective privacy policies:

  • TikTok Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy (YouTube)
  • Meta Data Policy (Instagram)

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through our official support channels.
