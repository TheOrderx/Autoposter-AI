Privacy Policy for Autoposter AI

Last Updated: May 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Autoposter AI ("we," "our," or "the App") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our services, including our website and our automation tools integrated with TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube.

1. Information Collected by Autoposter AI

When you use Autoposter AI, we may collect certain information to provide our automation services:

  • Account Information: Profile nickname and ID obtained via TikTok/Instagram/YouTube APIs to identify your account.
  • Media Content: Videos and captions you choose to process and publish through Autoposter AI.
  • Usage Stats: Follower counts and basic engagement stats to show you performance metrics within our dashboard.

2. How Autoposter AI Uses Your Data

We use the data obtained solely for the following purposes:

  • To enable the core functionality of Autoposter AI: automated video publishing.
  • To display your account profile within the Autoposter AI dashboard for management.
  • To optimize video content (resizing, metadata stripping) before it is posted.

3. Data Sharing and Third-Party APIs

Autoposter AI interacts with official Third-Party APIs (TikTok for Developers, Meta Graph API, Google YouTube API). Your data is shared with these platforms only to fulfill your request to publish content. We do not sell your data to third parties.

4. Data Retention

Autoposter AI does not store your raw social media passwords. We use secure OAuth tokens. You can revoke Autoposter AI's access at any time through your respective social media account security settings.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about how Autoposter AI handles your privacy, please contact us through our official support channels.
